WeStep Support

Need help with WeStep? We’re here to help.

Contact: support@updates.ezekielsung.com

1. Account help

If you’re having trouble signing in, creating an account, or updating your profile, please email us with a brief description of the issue and any relevant screenshots. Include the email address associated with your WeStep account so we can help you faster.

2. Password reset

On the sign-in screen, tap “Forgot password?” and follow the instructions. A password reset email will be sent to the email address on file. If you don’t see the email, check your spam folder. If it still doesn’t arrive, email support@updates.ezekielsung.com.

3. HealthKit and step data

WeStep reads daily step counts from Apple HealthKit. If your steps aren’t showing up:

  • Open the iOS Settings app → Health → Data Access & Devices → WeStep and make sure Step access is enabled.
  • Make sure your iPhone is recording steps (check the Apple Health app).
  • Try closing and reopening WeStep.

You can revoke or change HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings or the Apple Health app.

4. Friends and leagues

You can add friends, accept friend requests, and join leagues from within WeStep. If you’re running into trouble with friend requests, league invites, or leaderboards, email us and we’ll take a look.

5. Notifications

WeStep can send push notifications for friend requests, reactions, messages, sync requests, and league updates. If you’re not receiving notifications:

  • Open the iOS Settings app → Notifications → WeStep and confirm notifications are allowed.
  • Check your in-app notification settings within WeStep.

6. Account deletion

You can delete your account directly in the app:

Open WeStep → Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

If you can’t access the app, email support@updates.ezekielsung.com from the email address associated with your account, and we’ll help process your deletion request.

7. Privacy questions

For privacy questions, please review our Privacy Policy or email support@updates.ezekielsung.com.

Response times

We do our best to reply quickly. Response times may vary, especially on weekends and holidays. Thanks for your patience.
