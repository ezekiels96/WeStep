Your health data stays respected

WeStep reads step count from Apple Health only after you give permission. WeStep does not write to Apple Health, does not sell Health data, and does not use Health data for advertising. You can revoke access anytime in iOS Settings.

FAQ

Quick answers

Does WeStep use Apple Health?

Yes. WeStep reads your step count from Apple Health after you give permission. It doesn't write to Health and doesn't sell Health data.

Can I compete with friends?

Yes. Add friends, create Leagues, invite people, and compete on weekly step leaderboards.

Can I join a League without being invited directly?

Yes. You can join with a League invite code shared by a friend or organizer.

Does WeStep have badges?

Yes. Unlock badges for steps, streaks, weekly totals, social activity, and League progress.

Is there a Home Screen widget?

Yes. Add the WeStep widget to keep steps, goal progress, distance, and calories visible throughout the day.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Open WeStep → Profile → Settings → Delete Account. If you can't access the app, email support@updates.ezekielsung.com.

WeStep is a social step tracker for Apple Health, built for friends, Leagues, badges, widgets, and everyday movement.
